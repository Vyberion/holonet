ARTICLE I

Order Regulations

Regulation I

The Sith Codex is to be followed by everyone at all times.

Regulation II

Sith are to obey the commands of Overseer+ or Division Members when valid and lawful.

Regulation III

Team Killing is only permissible in the act of self defense, or while in the proper areas (dueling mats or outside of the temple).

Sub-Section I

Conscripts ranking Division Member and Sith Overseer+ are permitted to kill Sith when enforcing the codex.

Regulation IV

Sith must treat others with respect, regardless of rank.

Regulation V

Correct morphs must be worn at all times, unless given specific permission by the host of an event, or a member of High Command.

Sub-Section I

Contractors, Architects, and Supporters are exempt unless acting in an official capacity.

Regulation VI

Sith Codex violations are to be reported via #ticket-support in the communications server, or to on planet Law Enforcement (Dark Honor Guards, or Inquisitors.)

Regulation VII

Sith are forbidden from joining the Raider or Off-Worlders teams, unless given specific permission by event hosts or High Command.

Regulation VIII

Academy Student+, Supporters, and Contractors may freely enter and exit the temple.

Regulation IX

Exploiting is strictly prohibited and will result in a permanent ban.

Regulation X

All forms of trolling are prohibited and will result in punishment.

Sub-Section I

Severe offenses are punishable via execution, imprisonment, or banishment, based on the scale of the offense.

Regulation XI

Sith ranking Academy Student+ are to use grammar, syntax, and act in a mature manner at all times.

Regulation XII

Illegitimate events are forbidden, High Ranks are to adhere and follow the 'HR General Handbook' when hosting.

Regulation XIII

Permission to spectate trainings or trials must be granted by the event host. High Ranks, Division Members, and Supporters may freely spectate unless dismissed by the event host.

Regulation XIV

Permission to spectate Divisional Tryouts may be granted by the Commander or Overseer of said division.

Sub-Section I

Dark Councilors may spectate Divisional Tryouts freely, with the exception of Inquisitorial Tryouts. High Command and Inquisitors may spectate any event freely.

Regulation XV

Sith are to kneel to members of High Command, bow to Dark Councilors until told to rise, and must bow swiftly to High Ranks.

Sub-Section I

Those of superior rank do not bow or kneel to those of inferior rank.

Sub-Section II

Dark Councilors and above may bow to High Command till told to rise.

Sub-Section III

Project Managers and Ownership are only to be knelt to when morphed.

Regulation XVI

Sith Overseer+, Senior Reaver+, Senior Inquisitor+, Guard Lieutenant+, Dread Masters, and Supporters may at-ease or remove their helmets.

Sub-Section I

High Ranks require permission to at-ease or remove their helmets from DC+ when present.

Sub-Section II

Shadow Guards may at-ease or remove their helmets when ranked Guard Lieutenant+.

Regulation XVII

Usage of multiple accounts is prohibited. Alternative accounts must be authorized by High Command.

Regulation XVIII

Intentionally glitching yourself or others is prohibited. 'Glitching' is defined as the intentional misuse of tools or abilities in an attempt to create disarray.

Regulation XIX

Podium access is only granted to Dark Council+.

Sub-Section I

High Ranks and Division Members are permitted to remove trespassers from the podium.

Sub-Section II

Division High Ranks and Lord+ may go on the podium while hosting events.

Regulation XX

Exploiting loopholes in the Sith Codex is prohibited and should be reported via suggestions the communications server.

Regulation XXI

Avatar items that conflict or collide with morphs or tools are prohibited.

Regulation XXII

High Command has supreme authority over group function.

Sub-Section I

The Emperor's Wrath has supreme authority over Divisions.

Sub-Section II

The Emperor's Voice has supreme authority over the Dark Council.

Regulation XXIII

Spawn camping or killing is prohibited. Spawns are identified by capture points.

Regulation XXIV

Display and usernames are to comply with Roblox Terms of Service.

Regulation XXV

Event Hosts are permitted to set reasonable regulations for the duration of the event.

Regulation XXVI

Trespassing into locked halls is prohibited. Locked halls can be identified by the presence of announcements in the communication server.

Regulation XXVII

Sith and Division High Ranks may grant admittance into their respective rooms.

Regulation XXVIII

An inactivity notice may last no less than two (2) days and no more than one (1) month.

Regulation XXIX

Custom Morphs may be freely worn by all ranks, except for current Division Members.

Sub-Section I

Division Members may wear custom morphs to publicly hosted events (CTs, MMPs, FFAs, Tournaments, Raids, Practice Raids, etc.)

Sub-Section II

In the event a Dark Councilor or member of High Command prefers their custom morph to a group assigned morph, they may freely do so.

ARTICLE II

Regulation II

Apprentices must hold the rank of Sith Apprentice or superior.

Quantity of apprentices is determined by rank. One: High Command, Sith Marauder, Sith Seer. Two: Darth Mortis, Darth Baras, Sith Master, Sith Overseer. Three: Darth Acina, Darth Aruk, Darth Jadus, Darth Marr, General Malgus, Darth Nox, Darth Ravage, Darth Vowrawn, Darth, Sith Lord.

Apprentices require the approval of their master to be released from their tutorship.

Sub-Section I

A denied request allows the apprentice to invoke their right to a trial by combat.

Regulation V

Apprentices of Dark Councilors or High Command are permitted to accompany their master to any areas their master is authorized to access.

ARTICLE III

Kaggath Regulations

Regulation I

To be valid, Kaggaths must be authorized by the appropriate party.

Sub-Section I

Kaggath documents targeting Sith ranked Darth and its equivalents or beneath are to be submitted to the Warmaster and voted on by the Dark Council.

Sub-Section II

Kaggath documents targeting Dark Councilors are to be submitted to The Emperor's Voice.

Sub-Section III

Kaggath documents targeting High Command are to be submitted to the Project Manager.

Regulation II

Kaggaths may be justified only by a select set of reasons, including incompetence, abuse, immaturity, corruption, and inactivity.

Sub-Section I

A Humiliation Kaggath may be officiated solely off the basis of insult or disdain, with merit.

Regulation III

Kaggath documents must be written solely by the challenger without the use of artificial intelligence or third-party assistance.

Regulation IV

Sith ranking Sith Apprentice+ may attend Kaggaths.

Regulation V

There are three officially recognized types of Kaggaths: Ascension, Humiliation, and Domination. Powerbase vs Powerbase is the default Kaggath game mode. Both parties may settle and agree on an alternative game mode.

Sub-Section I

An Ascension Kaggath is a formal challenge with the intent to claim the opponent's rank or position, within three (3) ranks or two (2) ranks above the plaintiff's current rank. i.e. A Senior Guard may Kaggath a Guard Captain, but not the Guard Commander.

Sub-Section II

A Humiliation Kaggath is issued to disgrace or discredit the target. These may be fought as 1v1 duels or with group-wide support, where any Sith Apprentice+ may participate.

Sub-Section III

A Humiliation Kaggath may be issued against any individual, regardless of division/rank.

Sub-Section IV

A Domination Kaggath is a large-scale conflict sanctioned by the Dark Council, pitting entire powerbases or Divisions against one another to assert dominance. Only members of the Dark Council may authorize this type of Kaggath.

Regulation VI

Kaggath victors may determine the fate of the losing party.

Sub-Section I

Repercussions for defeat include: demotion, public humiliation.

Sub-Section II

Forfeiture will result in a demotion to the rank of Grotthu, and a one week rank-lock. Alternative forfeiture terms are prohibited by the opposing party.

Regulation VII

The Emperor attains a grace period of 3 weeks upon taking the throne.

Regulation VIII

Upon submission of a Kaggath, a mandatory 1-day buffer period is set in place before the event to allow for review.

Regulation IX

Submitting multiple Kaggath documents within 7 days is prohibited.

Regulation X

Unofficial Kaggaths targeting High Command are treason.

Regulation XI

Divisional entry ranks are prohibited from submitting Kaggaths.

Sub-Section I

Dread Host Kaggaths against Dread Masters are permissible.

Regulation XII

Withdrawing from a Kaggath after submission to the appropriate party is considered a forfeit.

Regulation XIII

A mandatory wait time of one week is to be completed before submitting a Kaggath, at the plaintiffs given rank.

Regulation XIV

Conscripts recruited to a division or Powerbase after the date of a Kaggath's approval are prohibited from participating.

Vouch Regulations

Vouches are to be used for trial requirements and trial re-take requirements.

Sub-Section I

Dark Council vouches may be used alternatively to normal vouches, including Combat, Lore, and Codex vouches respectively.

Sub-Section II

Regulation II

Sub-Section I

Acquiring vouches in knowledge based events requires a flawless performance.

Regulation III

Division High Ranks, Lord+, and Dread Masters are permitted to vouch for Sith.

Regulation IV

Vouches are invalidated once applied.

Sub-Section I

Vouches are invalidated after one month if not applied.

Regulation V

Sith ranking Dark Council or higher may not receive vouches.

Regulation VI

Vouches cannot be used to bypass trial failure cooldowns.

Regulation VII

Relinquishing vouches to other Sith is prohibited.

Regulation II

ARTICLE VI

Division Regulations

Regulation I

Conscripts must choose between Division or High Rank enlistment.

Regulation II

Sith of any Division or at any High Rank are to remain on duty at all times.

Sub-Section I

Division or High Ranking conscripts may temporarily go off-duty within their chambers.

Regulation III

Sith must be Academy Student+ to tryout for a division.

Sub-Section I

Specialized Divisions may have additional or alternative requirements.

Regulation IV

Division Members are bound to professionalism and appropriate conduct.

Regulation V

A Division Commander or Overseer may grant ranks upon the discharging of conscripts.

Sub-Section I

Honorably discharged conscripts may be granted a rank no higher than their equivalent and must server at least 2 weeks within their Division, honorably.

Sub-Section II

Dishonorably discharged conscripts are forcibly removed or abandon their Division before their minimum 2 weeks of service.

Sub-Section III

Dishonorably discharged conscripts may be ranked no higher than Academy Student.

Regulation VI

Division Members are bound by the Oath of Silence, only permitting them to speak within their respective team chats or whisper. Oath of Silence may be temporarily lifted by authorized event hosts, the designated Division Overseer, and High Command.

Sub-Section I

Conscripts ranking Senior Reaver+, Full Inquisitor+, Guard Lieutenant+, and Dread Master+ are free of their Oaths of Silence.

Regulation VII

Acts of minor disrespect toward Division Members are to be ignored.

Sub-Section I

Action may be taken, if an individual makes racist or profoundly offensive remarks.

Regulation VIII

Division Members are required to fulfill their weekly quota and requirements, to be determined by current Division leadership.

Regulation IX

Division High Ranks and Dread Masters are permitted to host trainings and trials for Low Ranks and Middle Ranks.

Sub-Section I

Senior Reaver+ may host Combat Trainings.

Sub-Section II

Senior Inquisitor+ and Guard Lieutenant+ may host Codex Trainings.

Regulation X

Division Members must acquire permission from the highest ranking conscript present to remove their helmets or at-ease.

Regulation XI

Users may be blacklisted from Divisions upon being dishonorably discharged, or for any reason the Commander or Overseer deems fit.

Sub-Section I

Upon being dishonorably discharged from a Division, you will be blacklisted for 2 weeks. Further duration is up to the Commander or Overseer, depending on the severity.

Sub-Section II

High Command may issue indefinite blacklists for Divisions, and may remove user blacklists from Divisions at their own discretion.

Sub-Section III

High Command may blacklist people from all Divisions at their own discretion.

Regulation XII

Division Members are required to log a minimum of 2 hours per week on planet.

Sub-Section I

Division High Ranks must host at least 2 Division Events (trainings or tryouts) per week.

Sub-Section II

Division High Ranks must host at least 1 TSO Event per week.

Darth Mortis retains absolute authority over the Dark Honor Guard, with the exception of the Emperor's Wrath.

Regulation VI

Dark Honor Guards require permission to go off-duty from the highest ranking officer present.

Regulation VII

The Dark Honor Guard is not restricted to a distinct amount of Sith.

Regulation VIII

Conscripts ranking Sith Overseer+ are not to be detained or arrested without explicit permission from High Command.

Regulation IX

Lieutenants and above may step onto the podium in the Guard Chambers.

Sub-Section I

Senior Guards may utilize the podium when hosting events.

Sub-Section II

Full Guards may go on the podium whilst assisting an event.

Regulation X

Conscripts ranking DHG Lieutenant+ and High Command may issue orders to Shadow Guards.

Sub-Section I

Shadow Guards are equivalent to Senior Guards but are capable of progressing to Guard Lieutenant while retaining their title.

Sub-Section II

Whilst guarding High Command, Shadow Guards may not be commanded by other entities.

Sub-Section III

Only worthy conscripts ranking Senior Guard+ may be admitted into the Shadow Guard, Champion of the Sith winners may also be granted admittance upon passing a Guard Tryout.

Sub-Section IV

Shadow Guards are to preform traditional guard duties when High Command is absent.

Regulation XI

Shadow Guards are to primarily guard members of High Command, prioritizing the DHG VIP list second.

Regulation XII

The maximum rank that may be granted upon honorably discharging is determined by position. DHG Initiate: Sith Apprentice. Full Guard: Sith Warrior/Sith Sorcerer. Senior Guard: Sith Marauder/Sith Seer. Guard Lieutenant: Sith Master. Guard Captain: Sith Lord. Guard Commander: Darth.

Sub-Section I

Shadow Guards are equal to Senior Guard, or Sith Marauder/Sith Seer, but are capable of ascending to Guard Lieutenant, being equal to Sith Master.

Regulation XIII

Shadow Guards are permitted in all rooms and restricted areas while guarding their VIP.

ARTICLE XI

Division and High Ranking conscripts may use the Force against rule-breakers. Using the Force on Division and High Ranking members is not allowed.

Sub-Section III

Dark Councilors are allowed to use the Force on disrespectful individuals.

Sub-Section IV

Guards are allowed to use Force Restrain on rule-breakers.

Regulation II

Interfering or disrupting events with the use of the Force is prohibited.

Regulation III

Sith are allowed to use Force Pull on Raiders that are scaling mountains, walls or in hard-to-reach areas.

Regulation IV

Violation of this article is considered 'Force Abuse'. Breaking the regulations will result in punishment, including removal of the Force gamepass without refund.

Regulation V

Using Force Teleport to escape combat is not allowed.

Regulation VI

Force usage outside of the Temple is unregulated and permitted on all conscripts. Raiders must initiate contact with Force before Sith may reciprocate.

Sub-Section I

Conscripts may only utilize Gamepass & Divisional/Departmental Force while outside of the Temple.

Sub-Section II

Conscripts are not to interfere with divisional duties while using the Force outside.
