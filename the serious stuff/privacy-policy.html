What We Collect

When you log in with Roblox, the Holonet stores your Roblox user ID, Roblox username, Roblox display name, and a server-side session record. The session record is used to keep this browser logged in and to check access to restricted Registry resources.

How Access Is Checked

Restricted pages check your current Roblox group ranks through Roblox group data. The site does not use email verification, passwords or email-based account recovery.

Cookies And Sessions

The Holonet uses a secure session cookie to identify your current browser session. Logging out deletes that session from the database and clears the cookie for this browser.

Analytics

The site may use Vercel Analytics to understand basic site usage and performance. Analytics are used for maintenance and improvement, not for selling personal data.

Data Sharing

The site is hosted on Vercel and stores login/session data in Supabase. Roblox is used for identity and group-rank checks. Data is not sold.

Retention

User records are kept so repeat logins do not create endless duplicate accounts. Sessions expire automatically and may also be removed when you log out.
